About

Jake Wells is a Kansas City-based artist blending alt pop, neo soul, and R&B into something that feels both familiar and uncharted. His music lives in the space between late-night introspection and daytime optimism — warm synths, honest vocals, and melodies that stick.

His music has been featured on HBO's FBoy Island ("Far From Home"), and his debut album WELLS arrived February 2026 via Jake Wells Music, LLC.
