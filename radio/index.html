About

Grateful receiver of musical intuition. Generally neurotic. Prefer rain. Not cool. Born in Florida. Sang in churches. Studied composition in Colorado. Dropped out before I learned how to make happy songs. Sang on The Voice for Adam Levine. Started a company making custom songs as a kind of alternative therapy (Salt & Water, Goodbyes, Julia, ROSEGOLD, sunshine garden, GRAND ADVENTURE). Licensed a song to HBO. Released debut album WELLS in February 2026.

The Album

WELLS — twelve tracks of alt pop and neo soul — was released February 23, 2026 on Jake Wells Music, LLC. "Lie to Me" is the only single released prior to the full album.
